Our Heritage

Rooted in the cultural heart of Kolkata, Gouri Jewellers stands as a testament to unparalleled craftsmanship and artistry. For generations, we have dedicated ourselves to the mastery of intricate jewelry design. We specialize in preserving traditional styles, bringing to life breathtaking filigree, exquisite openwork, and timeless gold ornaments that celebrate life's most precious moments.


Every piece in our collection is more than an accessory; it is a meticulously designed legacy passed down from our artisans to your family.
